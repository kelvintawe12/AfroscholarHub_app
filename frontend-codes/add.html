    • Welcome to MESHS school

    Montessori Higher Secondary School was started in the year 2003 by Our Founder MADAM TAWE GRACE NFAH Educational Trust with a very humble beginning and greater cause to provide quality education to all the rural students within the vicinity of the school and nearby villages.

    Montessori English Secondary and high school(meshs) is undoubtedly one of the best schools in santchou in terms of providing quality education to the students and ensuring that the parents are not burdened with the rising cost of education.

    Montessori English Secondary and high school(meshs) have been achieving 100% results in the 10th & 12th examination due to the sheer hard-work and commitment of our dedicated teachers backed by supportive management.
